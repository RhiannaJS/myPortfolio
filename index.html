About Me

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio! Let me tell you a little bit about my journey here. I attended the John Herron School of Art (while it was still a part of IUPUI), where I studied fundamental art, color theory and art history. I really enjoyed what I was doing, but learned rather quickly that I wasn't likely to make a decent living in the mid-west as an artist.

I took a bit of a turn and enrolled at Professional Careers Institute (aka Kaplan College) where I became a certified Dental Assistant. I had a career in dentistry for over 20 years. During that time I pursued as much education as was possible. I atteneded multiple courses involving esthetics. I also obtained my Dental Radiographer License, as well as Expanded Duties and Coronal Polish and Fluoride certificates.

I really have enjoyed the time I spent in dentistry, however, I am ready for a change. I am a recent graduate of Eleven Fifty Academy where I went through a 12-week immersive learning program for Web Development. I have spent more than 500 hours of logged coding time working on real-world projects while learning at the same time. I am passionate about coding and am a very hard working determined individual ready to start my new career.
